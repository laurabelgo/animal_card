Humpback Whale

Humpback whales have some of the longest migrations than any other mammal in the world. You could say they love to travel.

  • Scientific Name: Megaptera novaeangliae
  • Average Length: 49 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 45 - 50 years
  • Habitat: All oceans around the world.

Humpback whales are known for saving seals from killer whales. They are big softies.
